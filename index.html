Piatto History

More than 30+ years of experiance

Welcome to Piatto, where passion for Italian cuisine meets over 30 years of culinary expertise. Our journey began with a simple yet profound love for Italian flavors, and today, it's a celebration of tradition and innovation. From handmade pastas to wood-fired pizzas, each dish reflects our commitment to excellence and the finest locally-sourced ingredients. At Piatto, we invite you to savor the essence of Italy in every bite, amidst a warm and inviting atmosphere that's perfect for intimate dinners or joyful gatherings. Join us as we continue to redefine Italian dining, one unforgettable meal at a time. Buon appetito!
